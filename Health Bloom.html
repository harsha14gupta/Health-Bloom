ABOUT US

Shortage and poor quality of healthcare infrastructure at primary level is one of the major hurdles to building a robust system, and the situation is even worse in rural areas with limited supply of healthcare services. The continuum of care remains fragmented with primary care being totally disconnected from secondary and tertiary care.

Health Bloom aims to spread awareness about the prevention of various health issues as well as reduce the stigma and change the way we look at both physical and mental health. We focus on empowering the people to develop an understanding of the health sector in India and perspective on the challenges and opportunities. 

OUR PURPOSE

Bridging the information gap

Incorrect diagnosis in hospitals more than often leads to tragic deaths, due to wide information gap between doctors and patients, which makes the patients susceptible to whatever the doctor recommends, not knowing any better.

Spreading Awareness

On an individual level, there are numerous forces that influence one’s health- activity, family, food, health care access, coping etc. and it is important to be aware about all the key factors for maintaining a healthy life.

Leading causes of health issues

Access to nutritious food is the key to sustaining life and promote good health. According to WHO, an estimated 600 million in the world fall ill after eating contaminated food and 4,20,000 die every year, resulting in the loss of 33 million healthy life years.

Prevention and Healthy Living

The prevention of health problems and illness is more important than treatment and medicine. It will help maintain a healthy lifestyle and save money on medical expenses in the future. A healthy population is happier and more productive.
